The Five Dysfunctions of a Team

Be the leader your team needs to keep delivering and growing.

  • Topic: Based on Patrick Lencioni’s book “The Five Dysfunctions of a Team,” learn how to build a cohesive team using 5 key behaviors
  • Provided material: A physical or digital copy of Patrick Lencioni’s book
  • Format: Ten (10) 45-minute sessions, allowing us to cover the whole book
  • Takeaways:
    • A practical understanding of the five foundational team behaviors;
    • Practical tools to improve in these five areas;
    • Self-assessments of your team’s situation;
    • Concrete steps to implement these behaviors.

Managing Up

A workshop for tech professionals to boost their organizational influence.

  • Topic: Improve your relationship with your manager by learning to adapt your approach and become more effective in your current role.
  • Provided material: None.
  • Format: A 2-hour session.
  • Takeaways:
    • What are the key challenges to managing up;
    • How to discover the needs and expectations of your manager;
    • Different strategies for managing upwards;
    • Influencing and persuasion techniques.
